Best Practices for AI Reconciliation

Start with High-Volume Accounts: Begin automation with accounts that have many transactions but predictable patterns (cash, AR, AP). Quick wins build momentum.

Run Parallel for Validation: Don't switch cold turkey. Run AI reconciliation parallel to your existing process for 1-2 cycles to validate accuracy.

Train on Historical Data: Feed the AI your last 12-24 months of reconciliations. This accelerates learning and improves initial accuracy.

Review and Approve Initially: Even when AI is 95% confident, review matches in the first few cycles. Your approvals train the system on your specific business rules.

Monitor and Optimize: Track automation rates and exception patterns. Adjust matching rules and thresholds based on results. Continuous improvement is key.
