The Month-End Close Challenge

Traditional close processes suffer from:

Manual Coordination: Controllers spend hours coordinating tasks across teams, tracking completion, chasing down stragglers.

Sequential Dependencies: Tasks wait in queue because dependencies aren't met. One delay cascades through the entire process.

Exception Handling: Every close has exceptions. Researching and resolving them consumes massive time.

Lack of Visibility: Controllers don't know real-time status. "Are we on track?" requires manual check-ins.

The Cost: A 10-day close with 5 FTE dedicated to close activities costs $35,000 monthly in labor alone. Plus opportunity cost of not doing strategic work. Plus stress and burnout.
