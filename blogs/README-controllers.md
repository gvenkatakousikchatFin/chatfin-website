AI-powered automation designed specifically for controllers and accounting managers.

Managing month-end close, reconciliations, journal entries, and team coordination while ensuring accuracy and meeting deadlines.

**Automated Reconciliations**

**Close Management**  
AI orchestrates entire close process - checklist, dependencies, escalations

**Journal Entry Automation**  
Recurring and predictable entries posted automatically

**Exception Management**  
AI investigates discrepancies and proposes resolutions
