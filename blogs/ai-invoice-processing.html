The Invoice Processing Problem

Traditional AP processing is painful:

Manual Data Entry: AP clerks manually key in vendor, date, amount, line items from each invoice. Slow, boring, error-prone.

3-Way Matching Hell: Matching invoices to POs and receipts is tedious. Discrepancies require investigation. Process bottlenecks.

Approval Delays: Routing invoices for approval takes days. Approvers don't respond. Vendors complain about late payment.

Duplicate Risk: Manual processes can't reliably detect duplicate invoices. Organizations pay the same invoice twice.

The Cost: Processing 1,000 invoices monthly at $15 per invoice = $180,000 annually. Plus late payment penalties, missed discounts, and vendor relationship damage.

How It Works: Invoice Journey

Step 1 - Receipt: Invoice arrives via email, portal, or scan. Capture agent ingests and classifies.

Step 2 - Extraction: OCR and NLP extract vendor, date, PO number, line items, amounts with 99.9% accuracy.

Step 3 - Validation: System matches to PO and receipt. Validates quantities, prices, totals. Flags exceptions.

Step 4 - Approval: Invoices route to appropriate approvers based on business rules. Auto-approved if under threshold and fully matched.

Step 5 - Payment: Approved invoices schedule for payment optimizing for discounts and cash flow. Payment file generates automatically.

Total time: 4-8 hours vs. 5-7 days. Zero manual data entry.
